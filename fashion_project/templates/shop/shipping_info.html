Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about how your order gets to you.

Where We Deliver

FashionHub delivers across Kenya, including major towns and surrounding areas.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Nairobi: 1–2 business days
  • Major towns: 2–4 business days
  • Remote areas: 3–5 business days

Shipping Fees

  • Nairobi: KES 200 – 300
  • Outside Nairobi: Calculated at checkout
  • Orders above KES X,XXX qualify for free delivery

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with delivery details.

Failed or Delayed Deliveries

If we cannot reach you, we will contact you to reschedule delivery. After multiple failed attempts, the order may be returned.
