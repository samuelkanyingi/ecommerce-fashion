Returns & Refunds

We want you to love what you wear. If something isn’t right, here’s how we handle it.

Return Eligibility

  • Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging
  • Proof of purchase is required

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Our support team will guide you through the process

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Exchanges

Exchanges are subject to stock availability. If your requested size or item is unavailable, a refund will be issued instead.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos. We’ll sort it out fast.
